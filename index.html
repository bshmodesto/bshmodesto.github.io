About:

Based in California's Stanislaus County, Better Speech and Hearing Modesto was founded with the goal of helping children with Speech-Language disabilities enjoy local events. We currently provide reduced price tickets for local baseball games as a way to raise awareness for better speech and hearing services available to today's youth.

Events:

In 2016, Better Speech and Hearing Modesto helped provide over 200 tickets to a local Modesto Nuts baseball game for children with Speech-Language disabilities and their families. We thank our donors for helping provide this unique opportunity to these families.

Please email us for more information on upcoming events.
